Introduction

I am a Quantum Algorithm Developer at Cambridge Consultants. In my current role, I work on developing, optimising, and analysing algorithms for early fault-tolerant quantum computers.

The purpose of this site is for me to provide information about myself and my work, and to share my thoughts on quantum computing, algorithms, and various other topics of interest. Currently, the nature of my work means that most of what I do doesn't get published in the traditional sense, so my aim for this site is for it to be a way to share the exiciting things I've worked on with the world. I also hope to use this site to document my thoughts on the various happenings in the field
